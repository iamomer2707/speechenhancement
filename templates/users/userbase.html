    About

    Model is basically trained just for 200 epochs and it gives reasonable results, smaller layered network is also used because of lack of computational requirements as model is trained on google colaboratory. It has been seen that by adding the skip connection in the model, it produces better signal than that of the model without skip connections, because skip connection helps conserving low level features and doesn’t let them vanish. While training RCNN, it has been seen that addition of dropout layer in the model leads to deterioration of the quality of signals because it drops out useful features too while dropping random features, which can’t be easily recovered and leads to distorted signal and also needs more time to converge to given set of weights as RCNN without dropout. Model is trained on different kinds of nonlinearities like elu, selu, prelu and relu , in which relu came out with most pleasing results because it is not prone to problem like vanishing gradients relu substantially reduces the computational cost for training of the net. This allows the training of larger nets with more parameters at the same computational cost, thus leading to higher capacity, and often higher test set accuracy as well. Different kinds of optimizations like sgd, adagrad, adam are applied on learning rate of 0.0005 in which adam was the winning candidate
